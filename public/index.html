Start Lifting Weights Today!

Weightlifting is a fantastic hobby, and it can be practiced safely by almost anyone.

For Newcomers (**if performed correctly**):

  • You'll lose fat and gain muscle!
  • You'll reduce your risk of injury!
  • You'll strengthen your bones and connective tissue!
  • You'll be more confident!
  • You'll have fun!

Consider trying some form of weightlifting or calisthenics. Whatever your goals are, there is a methodology out there to help you optimize for those goals.

However, before beginning, please consider our tips and precautions on the Before You Start Lifting Weights page.
