Before You Start Lifting Weights

Preliminary Tips and Precautions

Before You Leave:
  1. Talk to a doctor
  2. Talk to an athletic trainer or physical therapist
  3. Get some shoes
  4. Pack a bag
  5. Ask an employee at your local gym about the facilities and locker room
  6. Eat some protein
  7. Bring a water bottle
At The Gym:
  1. Lock up your belongings
  2. Warm up and stretch
  3. Remember to breathe
  4. Remember to take adequate breaks
  5. Remember to drink water
  6. Form > Weight
  7. Don't neglect muscle groups
After The Gym:
  1. Drink some water
  2. Eat some protein
  3. Stretch
  4. Don't lift weights for 48 hours
  5. Sleep
  6. Light exercise (walking, hiking, etc.) on off days
